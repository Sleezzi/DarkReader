! Your elements must not contain "#+@$|"
! Put "*" to represent an unknown element.
! Put a "!" at the beginning of the line to make a comment (DarkReader will ignore this line)
! Exemple:
! Name: #NAME|
! Supported site: +WEBSITE.COM|
! The author of the style: @YOURNAME|
! The style URL (please give the same name to the style as to style.css): $URL.COM/STYLE.CSS|
